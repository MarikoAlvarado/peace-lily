The Peace Lily aka Spathiphyllum

The Peace Lily is known as "The Bringer of Peace" and it grows in Southeast Asia, South and Central America as well as Colombia. The Peace Lily is a popular indoor plant because of the ease in caring for it and the reward of the large and lovely lilies it can grow.
